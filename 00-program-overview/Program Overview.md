# 0. Program Overview

The **GlobalBank Enterprise AI Transformation Program** is an end-to-end enterprise architecture case study that demonstrates how a multinational financial institution can design, govern, secure, implement, and operate a global **Enterprise AI Platform**.

The program connects business strategy, enterprise architecture, data management, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, regulatory compliance, and platform operations. It provides practical architecture models, governance frameworks, technical patterns, implementation roadmaps, and operational controls for enterprise AI adoption.

The repository is designed as a:

* Professional enterprise architecture portfolio.
* Reusable AI architecture reference model.
* Practical learning and experimentation environment.
* Financial-services transformation blueprint.
* Reference for enterprise AI governance and security.
* Foundation for architecture assessments and reviews.

---

## Architecture and Technology Domains

### 1. Enterprise Architecture

Aligns business strategy, capabilities, data, applications, and technology across the organization.

### 2. Business Architecture

Defines capabilities, value streams, stakeholders, processes, outcomes, and transformation priorities.

### 3. Data Architecture

Designs governed data platforms, models, pipelines, metadata, lineage, quality, and data products.

### 4. Cloud Architecture

Defines scalable, secure, resilient, automated, and cost-efficient multi-cloud environments.

### 5. Artificial Intelligence Architecture

Integrates AI models, enterprise data, applications, platforms, governance, and security controls.

### 6. Machine Learning

Builds predictive models to identify patterns, classify events, assess risks, and support decisions.

### 7. Generative AI

Uses foundation models to generate content, summarize information, and automate enterprise tasks.

### 8. Agentic AI

Creates autonomous agents that reason, plan, use tools, and execute actions under defined controls.

### 9. Retrieval-Augmented Generation

Grounds language model responses using trusted information retrieved from enterprise data sources.

### 10. Knowledge Graphs

Models relationships between customers, transactions, risks, policies, entities, and business concepts.

### 11. Model Context Protocol

Standardizes secure interaction between AI applications, enterprise tools, services, and data sources.

### 12. AI Governance

Defines policies, decision rights, controls, accountability, oversight, and AI lifecycle management.

### 13. Responsible AI

Promotes fairness, transparency, explainability, privacy, accountability, safety, and human oversight.

### 14. AI Security

Protects AI models, agents, prompts, data, tools, infrastructure, and workflows from emerging threats.

### 15. LLMOps

Manages language models, prompts, evaluations, deployment, monitoring, cost, and version control.

### 16. MLOps

Automates model development, testing, deployment, monitoring, governance, and continuous improvement.

### 17. Platform Engineering

Creates reusable internal platforms that accelerate secure cloud, data, AI, and software delivery.

### 18. Architecture Governance

Ensures solutions comply with principles, standards, reference architectures, controls, and roadmaps.

## Program Scope

The program covers the complete lifecycle of an enterprise AI initiative, from business strategy and capability analysis to production deployment, governance, security, observability, evaluation, and continuous improvement.

The scope includes:

1. Business strategy and transformation drivers.
2. Stakeholder identification and capability analysis.
3. Current-state enterprise architecture assessment.
4. Target-state architecture definition.
5. Data platform modernization.
6. Cloud platform implementation.
7. Machine Learning and Generative AI development.
8. Agentic AI and enterprise tool integration.
9. AI governance and Responsible AI controls.
10. Cybersecurity, privacy, and regulatory compliance.
11. MLOps, LLMOps, and platform engineering.
12. Production deployment and release management.
13. Observability and performance monitoring.
14. AI model and application evaluation.
15. Incident response and operational governance.
16. Continuous optimization and architecture evolution.
