# 🏢 3. Company Overview

**GlobalBank Corporation** is a fictional Tier 1 multinational financial institution created to represent the scale, complexity, regulatory obligations, and operational challenges of a modern global bank. The organization serves retail, commercial, corporate, and digital-banking customers across multiple regions, processing millions of daily transactions through interconnected physical and digital channels.

GlobalBank operates under a federated enterprise architecture model supported by a hybrid and multi-cloud strategy. Its AI operating model combines centralized governance, standards, security, and risk oversight with federated delivery across business units. This structure enables the bank to scale artificial intelligence initiatives while maintaining regulatory compliance, architectural consistency, operational resilience, and responsible AI controls.
