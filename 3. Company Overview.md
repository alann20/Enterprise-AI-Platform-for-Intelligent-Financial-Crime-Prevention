# 🏢 3. Company Overview

**GlobalBank Corporation** is a fictional Tier 1 multinational financial institution created to represent the scale, complexity, regulatory obligations, and operational challenges of a modern global bank. The organization serves retail, commercial, corporate, and digital-banking customers across multiple regions, processing millions of daily transactions through interconnected physical and digital channels.

GlobalBank operates under a federated enterprise architecture model supported by a hybrid and multi-cloud strategy. Its AI operating model combines centralized governance, standards, security, and risk oversight with federated delivery across business units. This structure enables the bank to scale artificial intelligence initiatives while maintaining regulatory compliance, architectural consistency, operational resilience, and responsible AI controls.

🔍 Corporate Profile Overview

The profile reflects an institution operating at significant scale, with 52 million customers, 38 million digital users, 95,000 employees, and more than 45 million daily transactions. This operational footprint requires highly available, resilient, secure, and scalable platforms capable of processing real-time financial events, protecting sensitive information, and maintaining service continuity across multiple countries and banking channels.

GlobalBank manages multiple petabytes of data and more than 120 AI initiatives annually through a hybrid and multi-cloud strategy. Its federated enterprise architecture model enables business units and regional teams to deliver solutions independently, while centralized AI governance establishes common standards for security, model risk, regulatory compliance, Responsible AI, data management, architecture, and operational oversight.
